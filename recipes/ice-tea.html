Ice Tea

Description

This is the ice tea that I almost sometimes make. I wanted to practice making list pages one more time so I'm really reaching with this one but we shall see what I can come up with.

Who doesn't like ice tea though right? Oh damn I forgot the flavour. Well get ready for the best miscellaneous flavour ice tea you can imagine.

Ingredients

  • 500g of whatever fruits you want to use
  • 100ml lemon juice
  • 100g of white sugar
  • 150g of brown sugar
  • 5 bags of Rooibos tea (I DON'T CARE WHAT ANYBODY ELSE SAYS - THIS IS THE BEST ICE TEA BASE)
  • 1L of water
  • Fridge

Steps

  1. Remove most of the skin from your fruits if the skin isn't super edible
  2. Boil water and steep teabags in it for 30 minutes
  3. Remove the tea bags and add the sugars and mix
  4. Reheat the water to a medium high temperature
  5. Mince the fruit and add to the water, mixing vigorously
  6. Re-add the tea bags
  7. Cover with plastic
  8. Leave to steep for 24 hours in room temperature
  9. Strain, throw in some ice cubes and drink
